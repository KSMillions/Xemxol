About ZEMXOL

Built on Integrity.
Driven by Excellence.

ZEMXOL PTY (LTD) is a dynamic and versatile South African company based in Mpumalanga, committed to delivering quality goods and services across multiple sectors.

Founded in 2020, ZEMXOL has built a reputation for reliability, affordability, and excellence — whether supplying essential commodities or executing small-scale construction projects. Proudly rooted in Mpumalanga, we operate nationwide across South Africa, bringing the same dedication to quality wherever our clients need us.
